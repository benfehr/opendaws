About

Open DAW is a community based project started to help bring DAW music learning to everyone, regardless of situation.


Music production is famously elitist, with even the simplest music set ups at home costing thousands. The aim of Open Daw is to make the same available for everyone.


Those who sign up will have access to all open studio days, group mentoring sessions, event tickets, and the opportunity to share their own work with the entire community of users.
